YumicCart

YumicCart is a demo e-commerce platform designed to showcase how a modern online shopping website like Flipkart or Amazon is structured. This project focuses on frontend design, user experience, and product presentation.

The goal of YumicCart is to demonstrate product listings, detailed product pages, cart interaction, and support flows in a clean and understandable manner.

Why YumicCart?

  • Clear product categorization
  • Dedicated product detail pages
  • Expandable descriptions and specifications
  • Mobile-friendly responsive layout
  • Scalable structure for future backend integration
