Apple iPhone – Complete Timeline

From iPhone (2007) to iPhone 17 Pro Max

iPhone (1st Generation)

Manufactured: 2007

The original iPhone was manufactured in 2007 and marked a turning point in the history of mobile phones. Unlike traditional phones with physical keyboards, this device introduced a full touchscreen interface controlled entirely by touch.

It combined a phone, an iPod, and an internet communicator into one device. Although limited by today’s standards, it laid the foundation for modern smartphones.

  • Display: 3.5-inch LCD
  • Camera: 2 MP rear
  • Processor: Single-core

Not available to buy anymore

iPhone 3G / 3GS

Manufactured: 2008 – 2009

The iPhone 3G and 3GS expanded Apple’s smartphone reach globally. These models introduced faster internet connectivity and the App Store, which allowed third-party developers to build applications.

The iPhone 3GS improved performance and camera quality, making the iPhone more practical for everyday use.

  • Display: 3.5-inch LCD
  • Camera: 3 MP (3GS)
  • Major addition: App Store

Not available to buy anymore

iPhone 4 / 4S

Manufactured: 2010 – 2011

The iPhone 4 introduced a new glass-and-metal design and the Retina display, which dramatically increased screen sharpness. The iPhone 4S later introduced Siri, Apple’s voice assistant.

These models helped define Apple’s premium design identity.

  • Display: 3.5-inch Retina
  • Camera: Up to 8 MP
  • Feature: Siri (4S)

Not available to buy anymore

iPhone 5 / 5S

Manufactured: 2012 – 2013

The iPhone 5 brought a taller display and a lighter aluminum body. The iPhone 5S made history by introducing Touch ID, allowing users to unlock their phones using fingerprint recognition.

  • Display: 4-inch Retina
  • Processor: First 64-bit mobile chip (A7)
  • Security: Touch ID

Not available to buy anymore

iPhone 6 / 6S Series

Manufactured: 2014 – 2016

These models significantly increased screen size, making the iPhone more suitable for media consumption. The iPhone 6S added 3D Touch, allowing pressure-sensitive interactions.

iPhone 7 / 8 Series
